Your Forces

Units & Platoons

Platoon type is determined solely by the current token count in a region — not by how those tokens arrived, or what they were before. This fluidity is fundamental to how the game works.

★ The Fluid Platoon Principle

If 1 infantry token moves into a region containing 2 artillery tokens, those 3 tokens are now Airborne — immediately, with all of Airborne's permissions and restrictions. Always ask: how many tokens are here, and what does that make them?

Airborne — Movement Rules

Airborne moves in two steps, each to an adjacent region. The two steps can go in any direction — the path need not be straight. The only restriction is that the final landing region cannot be directly adjacent to the starting region.

✦ Mountains

Airborne cannot pass through or over a mountain region unless one of your own Bases is already present there. Even with Base support, Airborne cannot safely end a turn as 1 token alone in a mountain.

Exactly 2 Steps — Not Adjacent

Airborne lands exactly 2 steps away in any direction. Adjacent hexes cannot be landed on. Mountains without a friendly Base block passage and landings.
