## Three-Check

Deliver three checks to win. No checkmate needed. Completely changes the value of forcing moves.


{{svg:three-check-board.svg "Three-Check — starting position"}}

**Board:** Standard 8×8.

**Setup:** Standard chess setup.

**Win conditions:**
- Checkmate (as normal), OR
- Deliver check three times. Each check is counted regardless of whether it was blocked, evaded, or interposed.

**Key strategy:** Pieces that give check frequently (Queen, Bishops on open diagonals) become much more valuable. Sacrificing material to deliver checks becomes a core tactic. Defensive play must prioritise minimising the number of times the King is checked, not just surviving.

**Attribution:** Traditional variant. Popularised on Lichess. Public domain.
