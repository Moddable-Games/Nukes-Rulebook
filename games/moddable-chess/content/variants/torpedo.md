## Torpedo Chess

Pawns can always double-move, not just from their starting rank. Dramatically accelerates pawn play in the middlegame and endgame.

**Board:** Standard 8×8.

**Setup:** Standard chess setup.

**Rules:** Identical to standard chess except:
- Pawns may advance two squares from ANY rank (not just their starting rank), provided both squares are empty.
- En passant applies whenever a pawn double-moves past an enemy pawn, regardless of which rank it starts from.
- All other rules unchanged.

**Win condition:** Checkmate.

**Key strategy:** Passed pawns become much more dangerous because they can sprint two squares at a time. Pawn endgames are sharply different — a pawn on the 5th rank can reach promotion in two moves instead of three. Blockading passed pawns is more difficult. Connected pawns rolling forward together are extremely powerful.
