Infantry Movement Diagrams

1 — Same-Biome Flood Fill

In one move, infantry reach every connected field — regardless of distance. Adjacent mountains are also reachable as a final step into a different biome. The mountain with no field neighbour cannot be reached.

2 — Can Enter, Cannot Pass Through

Infantry can move into a friendly Artillery region (merging platoon types). It cannot move through it. With mountains sealing the corridor, the two fields beyond are completely unreachable.
